# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
GGSIPU EDC ACM International Conference on Blockchain, Robotics, AI & Network Security 2026 participants, including international researchers, students, faculty, and industry professionals. They seek academic rigor, prestige, and seamless access to conference information, important dates, call for papers, tracks, speakers, and registration details.

## Product Purpose
To act as the flagship website for the GGSIPU EDC ACM International Conference on Blockchain, Robotics, AI & Network Security (BRAINS 2026), establishing academic trust, prestige, and global participation.

## Brand Personality
- Flagship international research conference tier (NeurIPS/ICRA/CVPR/USENIX)
- Academic trust, prestige, and rigor
- Cutting-edge tech, global participation

## Anti-references
- College websites (too simple or basic)
- Overly flashy or marketing-heavy landing pages that lack academic seriousness

## Design Principles
- **Academic first**: Prioritize research presentation and trust.
- **Readable**: Exceptional typography and high contrast.
- **Premium**: State-of-the-art visual aesthetics, smooth layout, polished interactions.
- **Minimal**: Let the research and information speak for themselves; avoid clutter.
- **Timeless**: Academic trust and rigor.
- **Research-focused**: Clear highlighting of tracks, deadlines, and submissions.
- **Motion with purpose**: Subtle micro-animations, never purely decorative.
- **Accessibility over aesthetics**: High contrast, keyboard navigability, reduced motion support.
- **Whitespace over decoration**: Open, readable spacing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG level AA compliance.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- High-contrast text colors and clean semantic HTML for screen readers.
